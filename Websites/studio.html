About My Studio

Here at Chiara Fernando's Piano Studio, I welcome all who wish to learn how to play the piano regardless of age or experience. Having my piano studio at my home, presents a safe environment for students to learn and explore.

I began my piano studio in 2016 and have had a variety of students ranging in age and abilities. Ensuring a well rounded background in music, I make sure to teach students about:

  • Music Theory
  • Sight Reading
  • Ear Training
  • Technique
  • Repertoire
  • Musicianship
  • Composition
Read More

I use a variety of different resources in my lessons depending on the needs of each student. I have used the Piano Adventure books to help students grasp the concept of notes, the staff, and encouraging a healthy posture and technique. If students are interested in taking piano exams, I then switch them to the Royal Conservatory of Music (RCM) books. I have had two students successful pass their RCM exam with First Class Honors.

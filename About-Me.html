About Me

Chiara Fernando started taking piano lessons when she was seven years old, and continued taking them for 12 years. She has studied at a variety of music schools including Arcadia Academy of Music, Merriam School of Music, as well as with private piano instructors.

After successfully completing her Grade 8 RCM piano exam with Honors she began teaching piano privately at home. She has now been teaching piano privately for 5 years and has students ranging from 4 years old to adults. Chiara has had two students successfully complete their RCM exam with First Class Honors. Read the full text...

In addition to playing the piano, Chiara also plays the saxophone, guitar and sings. Chiara started learning the saxophone when she was accepted into the Regional Arts Program at St. Elizabeth Catholic High School and continued her studies at the University of Toronto in the Music Education Program. She studied under Chelsea Shanoff and Wallace Halladay.

Chiara will be starting Teachers College in the Fall of 2020 where she hopes to be able to teach music at a high school and continue teaching piano privately.

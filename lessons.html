Lessons

Lessons range from 30, 45 and 60 minute lessons. Lesson times are generally based on age and level of the student. Beginner or younger students generally have 30 minute lessons. For older, or more experienced students, I would recommend either 45 or 60 minute lessons.

I start all my classes by giving all students a FREE TRIAL LESSON. The trial lesson is usually 30 minutes long and allows for a proper introduction. This gives us a chance to talk about what the student's goals are in taking piano lessons and what they wish to achieve. If a student has previous piano experience, I may ask the student to play a short snippet of a piece so that I can assess where their abilities are at.

In addition, piano lessons are conducted at my house which presents a warm and welcoming environment. This helps ease the stress of playing in a classroom, or more formal environment. For online lessons, I am using both Zoom and LearnCube. The student will get to chose their preference of the platform they would like to use for their classes. Lessons are generally held on the weekend, but can be changed if necessary.
